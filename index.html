Overtime premium exemption (OBBBA "No Tax on Overtime")

2026 federal law excludes the FLSA overtime premium — the "half" of time-and-a-half, capped annually — from federal income tax. It does not affect Oregon tax, Social Security, or Medicare. Most payroll systems apply this only at tax-filing time, not per paycheck — leave this off unless you've confirmed your employer reduces withholding for it directly.
